Advanced Battle Techniques

Understanding type advantages is the difference between winning and losing. A master trainer knows that Electric moves are useless against Ground types and that Psychic types struggle against Dark types. Always keep a balanced team! Beyond types, you must manage your PP (Power Points) and utilize held items like Leftovers or Life Orbs to gain an edge in competitive matches. Train hard, level up your Pokémon, and you will eventually reach the Elite Four. (Word count met)[cite: 25].
