Retail Therapy
Released: 2008 		Featured Track: "Retail Therapy" by ARDN.

Popular
Released: 2013 		Featured Track: "Popular" by The Weeknd.

Released: 2010 		Featured Track: "Airplane Mode" by Limbo.
