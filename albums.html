Movie Soundtracks & Features

Iron Man: Original Soundtrack
Released: 2008 		Featured Track: "Retail Therapy" by ARDN.
Fun Fact: Ramin Djawadi composed the score, focusing on heavy guitar to match Tony's rockstar vibe.
Iron Man 3: Heroes Fall
Released: 2013 		Featured Track: "Popular" by The Weeknd.
Fun Fact: This concept album featured alternative rock and indie artists inspired by the movie.
Iron Man 2: AC/DC Deluxe
Released: 2010 		Featured Track: "Airplane Mode" by Limbo.
Fun Fact: The entire soundtrack for the second film consists of songs by the rock band AC/DC.
