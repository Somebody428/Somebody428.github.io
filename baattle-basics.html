Mastering the Battle

Battling is the core of the Pokémon experience. You must understand type matchups to succeed. For example, Fire is strong against Grass, but weak against Water. Electric moves can easily take down Flying types, but they have no effect on Ground types. Every Pokémon can learn four moves at a time, so choosing a diverse moveset is vital. You should always carry items like Potions and Revives to keep your team in fighting shape during long treks between Pokémon Centers. Becoming a Champion requires patience, strategy, and a strong bond with your team members.

  • STAB: Same Type Attack Bonus makes moves stronger.
  • PP: Power Points limit how many times a move is used.
  • Evolution: Some Pokémon change forms when they level up!
