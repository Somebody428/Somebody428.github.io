Iron Man Movie Profile

A billionaire inventor and playboy, Tony Stark, is captured by terrorists and forced to build a devastating weapon. Instead, he creates a high-tech suit of armor to escape. After returning home, he refines the suit and becomes the superhero Iron Man.

I like how Tony Stark's ingenuity and redemption drive him to become a true hero. The film shows how technology and responsibility can both create greatness and demand personal sacrifice.

  • Tony Stark: The billionaire inventor.
  • Pepper Potts: Tony's trusted assistant.
  • James Rhodes: Tony's best friend.
  • Obadiah Stane: The main antagonist.
