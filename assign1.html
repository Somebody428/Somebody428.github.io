Iron Man Movie Poster

A billionaire inventor and playboy, Tony Stark, is captured by terrorists and forced to build a devastating weapon. Instead, he creates a high-tech suit of armor to escape. After returning home, he refines the suit and becomes the superhero Iron Man, using his genius and resources to protect the world while confronting his own ego and corporate rivals.
