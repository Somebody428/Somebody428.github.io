Welcome to the Kanto Region

Pokémon are mysterious creatures with a vast array of abilities. Some Pokémon live in the tall grass, others in the deep oceans, and some even in the caves of high mountains. As a new trainer, your goal is to catch them all and complete your Pokédex. This website is designed to teach you about the origins of your journey, the basics of elemental battling, and how to choose your very first partner. We hope this guide helps you become a Pokémon Master!
