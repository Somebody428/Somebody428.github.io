Iron Man Movie Profile

Iron Man follows Tony Stark, a billionaire inventor and genius engineer, whose life changes forever after being captured by terrorists. Forced to build a devastating weapon, he instead creates a high-tech suit of armor to escape. Returning home, Tony perfects the revolutionary Iron Man armor and uses his new powers to protect the world.

Fast Facts

  • Director: Jon Favreau
  • Music by: Ramin Djawadi
