Iron Man Movie Profile

Iron Man follows Tony Stark, a billionaire inventor and genius engineer, whose life changes forever after being captured by terrorists. Forced to build a devastating weapon, he instead creates a high-tech suit of armor to escape. Returning home, Tony perfects the revolutionary Iron Man armor and uses his new powers to protect the world. With the help of his loyal ally Pepper Potts, friend Lt. Colonel James "Rhodey" Rhodes, and inspiration from his father's legacy, he becomes a hero unlike any other.
